## What will I get for my membership?

Great question!

I have 3 tiers of memberships available, the same levels of access through Patreon, Mee6 and YouTube, there isn't any major difference between them.

Bronze supporters get loyalty badges and priority response to comments in videos, as well as supporting the work that I'm doing.

Silver supporters get the same, plus access to exclusive content such as live streams, live Q&A and early access to content.

Gold supporters get all the benefits of Bronze and Silver, but also private coaching through my private Discord channels.
